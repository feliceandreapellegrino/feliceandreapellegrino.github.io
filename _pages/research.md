I work across **Control Theory**, **Machine Learning**, **Robotics**, and **Machine Vision**,
with a focus on intelligent, autonomous systems where these disciplines naturally converge.
I am particularly drawn to the challenge of making systems operate with limited or no model
knowledge — from model-free and data-driven control, to learning-based approaches for perception
and decision-making. On the robotics side, I work on kinematics, visual servoing, and safe robot
operation in industrial environments.

**My current focus is on advanced, intelligent manufacturing and contributing to human-centred,
safe, and sustainable production systems, in line with the vision of Industry 5.0.**
